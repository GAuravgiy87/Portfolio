About Me

Hi, I'm Gaurav Singh, currently pursuing a Bachelor's in Internet of Things (IoT) at Dayalbagh Educational Institute, Agra. I'm passionate about cloud computing, web development, Game Developments and the intersection of hardware and software systems.

With hands-on experience in building real-time web apps, Games deploying local AI solutions, and integrating embedded data visualizations, I enjoy crafting tech that's both smart and scalable. My toolkit includes Python, JavaScript, Django, AWS, and Docker, among others.
